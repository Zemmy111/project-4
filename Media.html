About Us

St. Augustines College Du, a Catholic Co-educational intermediate Academy owned and run by the Augustinian Fathers in Nigeria. It was purposely established in September 2016 to break new grounds in human development most especially in character formation and academic excellence of Nigeria teenagers. Our College community is guided by the Gospel message of salvation in the tradition of Saint Augustine of Hippo, a genius whose contemplative life shed enlightenment upon the entire Catholic faith and gives great meaning and value to education today. Here at St. Augustines College Du, we seek to develop the mind and heart in line with our core values of: UNITAS: Community, unity & sharing of common life, VERITAS: Truth, honesty, integrity, and self -discipline and CARITAS: Love of God and neighbor. For more information, visit: staugustinescollegedu.ng
